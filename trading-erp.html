Common Challenges in Trading & Distribution Businesses

Trading businesses often face challenges in managing inventory stock accuracy, purchase price fluctuations, sales order delays, and distributor payments due to disconnected systems and manual processes. Lack of real-time visibility leads to stock mismatches, GST billing errors, delayed deliveries, and blocked working capital.

Inventory Stock Mismatch & Poor Visibility

Mismatch between system stock and actual godown inventory leads to stock-outs, overstocking, and loss of sales due to lack of real-time inventory tracking.

GST Billing Errors & Compliance Issues

Incorrect GST calculations, HSN code errors, and manual invoicing create compliance risks and financial discrepancies in trading operations.

Purchase Price Fluctuations & Margin Loss

Frequent changes in supplier pricing without proper tracking impact buying decisions and reduce profit margins in wholesale and distribution businesses.

Delayed Orders & Distributor Payment Issues

Slow order processing, delivery delays, and poor tracking of outstanding payments affect customer satisfaction and block business cash flow.
