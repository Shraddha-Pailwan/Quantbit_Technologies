01 — Challenge

Manual Processes Were Breaking Payroll Every Month

The client, a mid-size auto parts manufacturer in Pune, managed payroll for over 300 employees entirely through spreadsheets and manual data entry. Every month, the HR team spent 5–7 days calculating salaries, leave deductions, overtime, and statutory contributions — all prone to human error.

Errors resulted in delayed salary disbursement, employee grievances, and costly rework. Compliance with PF, ESI, and PT regulations was inconsistently applied, creating risk of penalties. There was no single source of truth — attendance data, leave records, and payroll figures lived in separate files across multiple team members.

!

"We were spending more time fixing salary errors than actually running HR. Every payroll cycle was a fire drill."

— HR Manager, Client Company
02 — Solution

Unified HRMS with End-to-End Payroll Automation

Quantbit deployed its HRMS platform, configured specifically for the client's shift-based attendance structure and statutory requirements. The implementation covered five core modules:

  1. 01
    Attendance & Leave Integration

    Biometric devices were connected directly to HRMS, eliminating manual attendance entry. Leave applications and approvals moved to a digital workflow.

  2. 02
    Automated Payroll Engine

    Salary structures, grade-wise components, deductions, and arrears were configured once. Payroll now runs in minutes — not days — with zero manual calculation.

  3. 03
    Statutory Compliance Auto-Compute

    PF, ESI, PT, and TDS are computed automatically each cycle based on updated slabs, with challans generated ready for submission.

  4. 04
    Employee Self-Service Portal

    Employees access payslips, tax declarations, and leave balances independently — cutting HR queries by over 60%.

  5. 05
    Role-Based Approvals & Audit Trail

    Every change is logged. Multi-level approval workflows ensure accountability and eliminate unauthorized edits.

03 — Results
